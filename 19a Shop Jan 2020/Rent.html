What's The Space?

The Space in Juhu, Mumbai is a versatile venue with 2 high-specification boutique studios and a garden. As well as our regular fitness classes, The Space is ideal for occasional or regular rent, for personal training, rehearsals, fitness & wellness events and workshops.

Tell me more..

We have 3 areas available, 2 indoor air-conditioned studios and an outdoor landscaped garden area. You can read the full specification for each area by clicking on Basement, First Floor or Garden under STUDIO.

I want to run a regular fitness class at The Space

Please note we only permit appropriately qualified instructors with the vision and energy to grow, to teach classes at The Space. If this sounds like you, then as a first stage please complete this short application form setting out your experience and expectations. We will get back to you if your application matches what we are looking for.

I want to sell or promote my product at The Space

Please email swati@thespacejuhu.in with your brand profile and product lookbook. Please note we only help sell or promote products we believe in, which means authentic, high quality, fairly priced and environmentally sensitive.
