Yoga Flow - Hatha, Vinyasa Flow & Yoga Circuits Flexi Package
with Dhara Vachharajani

Description

Wake up your body, mind and soul with early morning deep stretches, core activation and engaged breathing.

Monday - Hatha Yoga: more relaxed class where the asanas are held for longer. Expect deep stretches focused on hip opening and spinal flexibility. Work on muscular endurance finishing with deep guided meditation.

Wednesday - Vinyasa Flow: a vigorous class involving continuous transitions between asanas, synchronized with breath. Expect cardiovascular and strength training.

Friday - Yoga Circuits: a rapid suryanamaskar (sun salutation) circuit interspersed with ab-work and iron yoga using light weights for fat loss and toning.
