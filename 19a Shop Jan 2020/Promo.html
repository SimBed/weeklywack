Process

  1. Confirm your booking of the studio by making your payment
  2. Make a request for your preferred promotional services and provide the relevant content, as set out in the requirements
  3. We will confirm if the promotional services can be provided and if so raise an invoice for these services and set out any edits (if necessary) to the content to correctly meet our stated requirements
  4. Full payment for these services must then be received, before we will proceed further
  5. Content must be received reflecting any changes we have advised at least 10 days before your event
  6. Once both payment and correct content has been received, the content will be posted on a mutually agreed Wednesday before your event
  7. If we have not received payment within 10 days of the event, the services will not be provided
  8. If correct content has not been provided within 10 days of the event, the promotional services will not be provided and you will be refunded 50% of any payment made for promotional services

Terms & Conditions (Social Media Posts & Stories)

The Space will only post high quality content that has been pre-approved, at the discretion of The Space. All posts must meet the following requirements:

  1. Engaging, clear, relevant and honest
  2. Correct punctuation, grammar and spelling
  3. High quality, high resolution, non pixelated images
  4. Minimal & clean design
  5. Text on images and videos to be limited to under 20% of the total image area
  6. Videos must be under 45seconds. Any music used must be royalty free stock music
  7. Any image/visual consisting of a single-use plastic will not be promoted on any of our platforms
  8. All the content and visuals should be submitted 3 weeks prior to the event

Terms & Conditions (Instagram Live)

  1. The Space will provide the device to go live on The Space’s Instagram page
  2. The device may be used during the ongoing session for a maximum of 5 minutes
  3. The client will provide a representative from its team to conduct the Live
  4. Profanities, mis-representation of facts and false information are not permitted
  5. Content may only promote the pre-booked session and not any other product/service

Terms & Conditions (Contests)

  1. Content must be clear, engaging and pre-approved with The Space
  2. Prize must be clearly defined and pre-approved with The Space
  3. Correct content must be ready a minimum of 2 weeks before your event
  4. The Space will select all winners within an agreed timeline
  5. Delivery of contest prizes will be entirely at the client’s effort and expense
  6. A refundable deposit of the value of the prize must be made to The Space, fully returnable once the winner confirms they have received a prize, consistent with their reasonable expectation.
  7. Any image/visual consisting of a single-use plastic will not be promoted on any of our platforms

Terms & conditions (Promotions Onsite at The Space)

  1. The concept must be clearly communicated and pre-approved with The Space
  2. The concept should be engaging and creative
  3. The promotions should start a maximum of 2 weeks before your event
  4. Your representatives may respectfully promote the session but not be pushy, invasive or irritate members
  5. Single-use plastics in any form are not permitted
  6. Any activity not in line with the pre-approved concept will be immediately stopped without refund

General Terms

  1. The Space offers no guarantee in any way of the impact of the promotions on sales
  2. The Space does not provide any metrics to analyse the reach or impact of the promotions.
  3. In order to satisfy internal processes, The Space may at its discretion make any changes to your given content, without explicit approval, if it believes the changes have no material impact on the overall integrity of the post

Payment & Billing Terms

  1. 18% GST applies to all services
  2. Once payment is made, work will commence so payments are non-refundable regardless of subsequent changes in circumstances
  3. If we have not received payment within 10 days of the event, the services will not be provided
  4. If correct content has not been provided within 10 days of the event, the promotional services will not be provided and you will be refunded 50% of any payment made for promotional services
