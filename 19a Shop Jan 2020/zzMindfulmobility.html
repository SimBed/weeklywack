Description

An invigorating class tailored for your morning body. A class inspired by Ido Portal’s Movement Culture. Use the language of multiple disciplines like Yoga, Dance, Martial Arts and Gymnastics in a single practice designed to keep your nervous system charged and help you reach the full potential of the best machine ever built - your body!

Monday & Wednesday | Flex & Flexibility:
Use strength training & active flexibility to deepen your skills while focusing on injury prevention. Hello burns!

Friday | Monkey Movement:
Work on awareness, reflexes, co-ordination, mind-body connection and flow using partner drills, floorwork & locomotion. Build primal strength while increasing your movement to a range you’ve never explored!
