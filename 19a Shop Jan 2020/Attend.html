Attend a Class

What's on offer?

The Space offers a range of high quality fitness and lifestyle classes with qualified and experienced instructors in a clean, comfortable, peaceful environment.

Which class is for me?

Most classes offer trials at a favourable rate, so you can have the opportunity to try out a class. If you are unsure which class to try, The Space Unlimited Flexi Package 1 week trial may be ideal for you. For Rs.1,800 you can attend any of a wide range of listed classes.

You can read all about the classes we offer (including times and prices) by clicking on the different options under CLASSES.
Click on:

Try a Class under MEMBERSHIP to compare the benefits of all classes side-by-side.
CONTACT for specific class queries and call the instructor directly.
WHAT'S ON to find out about fun workshops and events each month.

If you still want help deciding which class is right for you, give us a call on 9892035182.

I'm ready for my first class!

Please call us on 9892035182 to book your spot and discuss any concerns you may have.

Payment must be made in advance of attending a class either online or at The Space reception. Payment methods will be explained on booking. You should arrive in good time to pay before class or ideally pay online in advance of arriving at The Space, to avoid any risk of delay before your class.

My next class

Wow, that was good! What about my next class? After the initial trial, if you like a class, you can decide to book a Package or Bulk-Buy for a set number of classes with a limited period of validity, just 'Drop In' from time to time or try a different class. Booking a Package usually works out best, as it ensures the instructor will keep a space for you, motivates you to attend regularly and works out more economical per class.

When to arrive

Get the most out of your class by arriving 5 to 10 minutes ahead of the scheduled class time, so you are relaxed when the class starts and you receive full benefit from the class. Please allow additional time if you have not yet made payment, as you will need to pay in full before attending your class.

Most definitely don't arrive late! If you arrive late, you disturb the class and if the instructor thinks it is dangerous for you to join late (because you have missed the warm-up or missed an important technical explanation) they will not let you join.

What shall I wear

Bring a pair of clean, well-fitting sports shoes to change into, as outdoor shoes are not permitted in the studios for hygiene. Or go barefoot if suitable for the class. Generally wear sportswear or comfortable, loose fitting clothing with long hair tied back. Some classes however have different requirements, described on the relevant page under 'CLASSES'. We recommend you do not bring jewellery to The Space. It is easily lost, can cause damage or injury and can sometimes interfere with specific activities.

Where do I go?

Enter The Space by the gate on A.B. Nair Road (next to Bayroute and directly opposite Chin Chin Chu), walk up the path and turn left into the lobby towards the blue door leading to reception. Our lovely receptionist will help you find the correct studio for your class, which may be in the basement, on the first floor or in the garden.

Payment

Full payment must be made in advance of attending a class either online or at The Space reception. Payment methods will be explained on booking. You should arrive in good time to pay before class or ideally pay online in advance of arriving at The Space, to avoid any risk of delay before your class.

Health concerns

If you have any specific health concerns, are new to exercise or have not exercised in some time, we recommend you speak to your doctor to check the suitability of your proposed class. Please also alert your instructor to any specific health issues you face.
