Packages at The Space

Package Terms

Packages specify a number of classes (which is either a fixed maximum or unlimited) and a period of validity:
  • Any full or part class attended counts as a class
  • The Package starts the day of attendance of the first class in the Package
  • The last day of validity of the Package is the earlier of:
    1. the date the maximum number of classes is attended (where applicable)
    2. the close of day on the day prior to the start date plus the period of validity

Freezing Packages

Packages/Trials of 1 week or less/Custom & Group Packages of all terms:
No freeze allowed

Packages of 1 month/5 weeks:
You may freeze your Package once. The freeze must be for a consecutive number of days of a minimum of 7 days and a maximum of 14 days. The freeze must be specified at the time of buying your Package. Once you have attended your first class, you may not freeze your Package if you have not previously specififed the dates at purchase.

Packages of 3 months or more:
You may freeze your Package twice subject to a maximum total freeze of 21 days. Each freeze must be for a consecutive number of days of a minimum of 7 days and a maximum of 21 days. You can freeze your Package at any time with at least 24 hours notice. You cannot retrospectively freeze your Package.

Note:

  • Your Package validity will be extended by the same number of days for which it was frozen
  • If you attend a class while your Package is frozen, it will immediately restart
  • If you attend a class while your Package is frozen, but before the minimum freeze period has passed:
    • your Package will be treated as active (not frozen) during your short period of absence
    • your remaining available freezes will be reduced by one
  • A Package is only frozen when a freeze has been requested in writing and The Space has confirmed the freeze in writing

Cancellations, Refunds and Restarts

Packages cannot be cancelled or refunded, however if after starting your Package you decide you do not wish to continue with it at that time, you may Adjust and Restart the Package as described in the next section.

Adjust & Restart

Packages cannot be cancelled or refunded, however if after starting your Package you decide you do not wish to continue with it at that time, you may (at any time before the normal expiry of your Package, but not after expiry) Adjust and Restart the Package in the following way:
  1. Request the Space to Adjust and Restart your Package;
  2. Make an associated payment for those classes, which you did attend in your Package, prior to requesting a restart (subject to a minimum payment of Rs.1,000). These will be treated as Drop In classes instead of Package classes. Your request to restart will be accepted once this is paid in full;
  3. Once the restart is accepted, restart the full Package from scratch at a date of your choosing within 1 month of requesting a restart. If the Package has not been restarted after this time it will expire permanently.

Classes Cancelled by The Space

Notification

We aim to avoid cancelling classes wherever possible, but inevitably there will be occasions where this is unavoidable.
We aim to notify regular participants of a class of a cancellation in good time. The most effective way to avoid the disappointment of turning up to a cancelled class is to join the class Whatsapp group and follow The Space Instagram stories on @thespace.juhu.

Packages for a Single Class (with a Fixed Maximum Number of Classes)

Where a class is cancelled, that you could reasonably be expected to have attended, we will extend the validity of your Package by at least an equivalent number of days.

Packages for a Single Class (with an Unlimited Number of Classes)

We will not extend the validity of your Package for occasional cancellations, however where a substantial number of classes have been cancelled, that you could reasonably be expected to have attended, The Space Management may, at its discretion extend the validity of your Package.

The Space Flexi Package (Occasional Class Cancellations)

We will not extend the validity of your Package for occasional cancellations, as the nature of the Package provides alternative options should a particular class be cancelled.

The Space Flexi Package (Removal and Addition of Participating Classes)

The timetable and participating classes are subject to change. Where such changes are substantial and have unreasonably changed the nature of the Package, The Space Management may, at its discretion agree an alteration to your Package to ensure you are not unfairly treated.

Bulk-Buys

Bulk-Buys are somewhat similar to Packages, however for a given number of classes, the validity period would be much longer than for an equivalent Package. The longer validity offers great flexibility, so the extra flexibility options available to Packages do not apply to Bulk-Buys, nor is the validity of a Bulk-Buy extended for an occasional class cancellation. For example, Bulk-Buys can never be frozen. Nor can Bulk-Buys be Adjusted and Restarted. Other than this, Bulk-Buys function very similarly to Packages.

Alterations to these Terms

Any communication you may have understood to indicate a difference to the terms laid out here should be presumed to be incorrect unless specifically stated in writing by The Space management.
