Sing Song Sign ™ (Baby Signing Basics Course)
with Chantal Norton

Description

A Baby Signing Basics 10 week course for parents and babies aged 6 months - 18 months. These musical classes are a real treat for parents and babies. We enjoy singing to beautiful children's songs, simple stories, dancing, toys and sensory props as we learn baby signing together. These classes can decrease crying and tantrums, enhance bonding and accelerate verbal-language acquisition.

Supercharge your child’s language development & communication before they can even say a word!
