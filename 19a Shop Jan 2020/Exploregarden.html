Additional Info for Regular Classes
Terms

Regular Classes must run on a weekly basis for a duration of 60-90mins with a minimum 6 months committment.
Appropriate instructor qualifications (copies of certificates to be provided)and praiseworthy testimonials are mandatory before starting Regular Classes.
To run Regular Classes on these terms, all instructors are required to first sign a Statement of Understanding with The Space, which sets out the details of working at The Space.

Additional Info for Workshops

Time & Costs

A minimum booking of 2 hours is required.
Booking over the minimum hours required will be charged by the hour according to the hourly rate above.
Costs are for the period of activity. Time for setup and takedown are in addition at the rate of 50% of the costs shown.
Any part hour is charged at the full hourly rate. For example a booking from 2.00 to 4.30 is charged as 3 hours.
Any extra use (full hour or part hour), outside of the agreement will be charged at the full hourly rate.

Deposit

A refundable deposit of 100% of your booking amount will required at the time of confirming your booking.
The deposit is refundable in full following the Event, subject to:
no damage; all Studio Usage Terms adhered to; hours used being no more than booked.

Confirmation & Cancellation

The booking is only confirmed once payment of 100% plus any deposit has been paid.
In the event of cancellation with at least 24 hours notice, 50% of the payment, plus 100% of the deposit will be refunded.
In the event of cancellation with less than 24 hours notice, only the deposit will be refunded.

Promotions

Promotion of your event may be provided if the topic of the workshop is in line with our brand identity. Based on the services required, promotions may be charged additionally to your booking. To discuss, please contact swati@thespacejuhu.in.

Studio Usage Terms

  1. Mindful of the environment we all share, we encourage guests to consider the impact of products brought to The Space. We encourage healthy, sustainable, considerate practices and strictly prohibit the use of 'one-use and throw' type products e.g. plastic or Styrofoam water bottles, plates, cups and cutlery, packaged foods and packaged drink. Please do not bring these on to the premises. The Space staff has been directed to immediately removed any such items seen on the premises.
  2. The Space provides Bisleri water coolers on site.
  3. Smoking is not permitted anywhere on the premises (either inside or outside).
  4. The Space staff is not available to provide additional support for your workshop, for example to assist with any set up and take down, decoration or guest relations. These services can be provided if agreed in advance at an additional charge.
  5. Any decoration that is intended to be attached to internal or external property in any way needs to be explicitly agreed by The Space management in advance.
